# User Accounts

User accounts in Animation Playground provide a personalized experience, allowing you to save, organize, and manage your animation configurations across devices.

## Creating an Account

### Sign Up Options

Animation Playground offers multiple ways to create an account:

**Email & Password**

1. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
3. Verify your email address (check your inbox)
4. Start creating and saving animations

**OAuth Providers**

- **Google**: Sign up with your Google account for quick access
- **GitHub**: Perfect for developers who want to link their coding identity
- **More providers**: Additional OAuth options may be available

<Callout type="info">
  All authentication is handled securely through Supabase Auth, ensuring your
  personal information is protected.
</Callout>

### Account Verification

After signing up with email:

1. Check your email inbox for a verification message
2. Click the verification link
3. Your account will be activated automatically
4. You can now sign in and start saving animations

## Managing Your Animations

### Personal Library

Once logged in, you gain access to your personal animation library:

**Saved Configurations**

- All your created animations are automatically saved
- Each animation keeps its configuration settings
- Animations are synced across all your devices
- Access your library from any browser where you're signed in

**Organization Features**

- **Custom Names**: Give descriptive names to your animations
- **Creation Dates**: See when each animation was created
- **Last Modified**: Track when you last updated each animation
- **Quick Preview**: Hover over animations to see a quick preview

### Animation Management

**Saving Animations**

1. Create or modify an animation in the playground
2. Click the "Save" button in the configuration panel
3. Enter a descriptive name for your animation
4. The animation is automatically saved to your library

**Loading Saved Animations**

1. Go to your profile page or library
2. Browse through your saved animations
3. Click on any animation to load it in the playground
4. Modify and re-save as needed

**Duplicating Animations**

- Create variations by duplicating existing animations
- Perfect for creating animation families with slight differences
- Maintains the original while allowing experimentation

<Callout type="tip">
  Use descriptive names like "Button Hover - Blue Theme" or "Card Entry -
  Mobile" to easily find animations later!
</Callout>

## Profile Management

### Account Settings

Access your account settings from the profile menu:

**Personal Information**

- Update your display name
- Change your email address
- Modify account preferences

**Security Settings**

- Change your password
- Enable two-factor authentication (if available)
- Review recent login activity

**Preferences**

- Set default animation duration
- Choose preferred export format
- Configure notification settings

### Privacy Controls

**Animation Visibility**

- **Private**: Only you can see and edit your animations
- **Public**: Others can view via share links
- **Unlisted**: Not searchable but accessible via direct links

**Data Management**

- Export all your animation data
- Delete specific animations
- Request complete account deletion

## Data Security & Privacy

### Data Protection

**Encryption**

- All user data is encrypted at rest and in transit
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard methods
- Sensitive information is never stored in plain text

**Data Location**

- Data is stored securely in Supabase's infrastructure
- Complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations
- Regular security audits and updates

### Privacy Rights

**Your Data Rights**

- **Access**: Request a copy of all your data
- **Portability**: Export your animations in standard formats
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data
- **Correction**: Update incorrect information

**What We Don't Do**

- We don't sell your data to third parties
- We don't use your animations for training AI models
- We don't track you across other websites
- We don't send spam or unwanted emails

## Account Recovery

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Follow the link to create a new password

### Account Recovery

For other account issues:

1. Try signing in with different OAuth providers if you used multiple
2. Check if your email address has changed
3. Contact support through GitHub issues
4. Provide as much detail as possible about your account

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't Sign In**

- Verify your email address is correct
- Check if caps lock is on for passwords
- Try password reset if you're unsure of your password
- Clear browser cache and cookies

**Animations Not Saving**

- Check that you're signed in
- Verify your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page and signing in again
- Check browser console for error messages

**Missing Animations**

- Ensure you're signed into the correct account
- Check if you used a different email address
- Verify you didn't accidentally delete the animation
- Contact support if animations are definitely missing

### Browser Compatibility

**Supported Browsers**

- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

**Known Issues**

- Some older browsers may have limited functionality
- Private/incognito mode may affect some features
- Ad blockers might interfere with authentication
